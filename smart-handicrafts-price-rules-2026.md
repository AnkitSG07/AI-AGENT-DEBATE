## Critical compatibility rules
- AS-B-201-SLD is a rechargeable 1-colour / single-colour driver. It is for single COB LED use, not dual COB / 3-colour use.
- Never recommend 2W+2W / 4W dual LED with AS-B-201-SLD.
- AS-B-202-DLD is the rechargeable 3-colour / dual LED driver. Use this for dual COB / warm-cool / 3-colour setups.
- Normal dual COB LED options are 3W dual COB and 5W dual COB.
- Do not present 4W dual COB as a normal dual COB option.
- LC Series AS-B-202-DLD-LC may be discussed only as an LC/cost-sensitive set if the customer explicitly asks for LC/cost-sensitive option.
