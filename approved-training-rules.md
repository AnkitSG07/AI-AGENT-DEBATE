# Approved Smart Handicrafts AI Training Rules

Rules in this file are approved for AI Mode and may be used in future replies.

- DRIVER - 205 STRIP LED DRIVER, SKU AS-U-205-LSD / AS-B-205-LSD wording depending on catalogue context, should be treated as the fast-charging rechargeable strip LED driver.
- DRIVER - 204 STRIP LED DRIVER should be treated as the normal/standard charging rechargeable strip LED driver.
- When comparing 204 and 205, explain that 205 is the fast-charging option and 204 is the normal charging option.
- For LC kits, 1200mAh battery should be the first recommendation because LC kits are cost-optimized.
- For normal rechargeable lamp kits, 2600mAh battery should be the primary/default recommendation.
- 5200mAh battery pack is a bigger sleeve pack, similar to two 2600mAh cells packed together, and should only be suggested when customer needs higher backup and has enough space.
- Do not recommend BMS battery automatically unless the customer specifically needs it.
